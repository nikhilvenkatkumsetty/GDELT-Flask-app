Foresight is essential in anticipating the future opportunities and risk in governmance. Policy makers provide institutions and governments with a foundation to guide and determine decision making and procedures. Theory of democracy states that the variety of attitudes and preferences of the public are a key to shape policymaking. In the volatilely changing environment of today, making accurate predictions becomes ever more challenging. Hence, the target of this project is to provide actors in governance with a tool that may aid foresight through

  • Public sentiment analysis
  • Trend detection
  • Predictive analysis
  • Correlation analysis
  • Casuality analysis
via
  • Text mining and temporal and geographic social characterization;
  • Machine Learning
  • Big data techniques
  • Causality inference
  • Time series forecasting
  • Bayesian inference
