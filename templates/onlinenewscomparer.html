News Comparer

News Comparer allows us to compare the results of multiple queries together or compare coverage of up to four separate queries in a single live-updating dashboard, or and display the results of one query as a percent of another to answer questions like what percent of coverage of a given topic also mentions a specific subtopic or framing. All of GDELT Summary are simply wrapped around the GDELT TV 2.0, DOC 2.0 and GEO 2.0 APIs using their JSONP output formats. That is that, we can use GDELT Summary as a template to create our customized live-updating dashboard and visualizations!
